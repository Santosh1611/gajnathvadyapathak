ABOUT US

'Gajnath Vadya pathak' is a group engrossed in cultural music and traditional instruments.

WHO WE ARE

'Gajnath Vadya Pathak' Pune was established in 2017. The idea of having a special class to bring in the culture of Maharashtra and retain the folk art form was implemented. The pathak performs on traditional Indian drums and instruments. It believes in bringing discipline to music. Gajnath was founded by 11 experienced shoulders using only 10 dhols and 4 tashas. Up until now, the count has reached 60 dhol and 18 tashas. At the very beginning, Gajnath had 30 members. The total number of members has now risen to 110, representing all age groups and including the active participation of women.

EVENTS

Gajnath has left its mark during festivals. They have successfully given 33 live performances in the localities of Pune. Their music and performance add to the liveliness and energy of the occasion. Till date, it has given performances at festivals like Ganeshotsav, Navratri, Janmashtami, etc. All the members of Gajnath work together to carry forward the significant cultural trait. Starting from 30 members, today we are a group of 110 total members and still counting.
